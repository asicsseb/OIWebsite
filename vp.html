Volunteer Points

The only thing that makes an organization like ours run is the unpaid time and effort of countless volunteers. One of the ways we as an organization make it up those volunteers is by rewarding them with points which they can redeem for various in game benefits. It is important to us that the benefits these players receive be as transparent and fair as possible, while also giving chapters some discretion in awarding points for in demand donations. Below is our volunteer point policy for awarding these points.



For running a live action roleplay event, a chapter accrues 100 volunteer points to distribute for material contributions to the game. Additionally a chapter may award volunteer points for people who donate their time to make the game better.



Volunteering (These points do not come out of a chapters discretionary pool)


---------------------------------


Work Outside of Events for the Organization - 20 VP/Hour


NPC - 25 HP per 4 hour shift, or 100 HP per event day


Staff(LM, NPL, Props) - 125 HP per event day


CD/HOP = 150 HP per event day



Donations (These points come out of a chapters discretionary pool. These are guidelines, and a chapter may award more or less depending on the needs of the chapter)


---------------------------------


Packets - 1 HP each


Weapons/Shields - 50-100 HP


Armor - 75-500 HP


Cash (or Cash Value of Donation) - 5 HP/$


High/Critical Demand can increase HP reward by 1.5-3x.
